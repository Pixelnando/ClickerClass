The [b]Clicker Class[/b] mod adds a set of weapons inspired by idle games like [i]Cookie Clicker[/i]. Show your enemies who's the boss by clicking them to death with a new line of weapons, the [b]Clickers[/b]. Add some silly spice to your full-game playthrough and prove who can click the fastest with the Clicker Class mod today!
